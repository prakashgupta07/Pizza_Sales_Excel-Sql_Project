KPI's REQUIREMENT
We need to analyze key indicators for our pizza sales data to gain insight into our busniess 
performance.Specifically, we want to calculate the following matrics:

1. Total Revenue: The sum of the total price of all pizza orders.

2. Average Order Value: The average amount spent per order,calculate by dividing the 
   total revenue by the total number of orders.

3. Total Pizza Sold: The sum of the quantities of all pizza sold.

4. Total Orders: The total of order placed.

5. Average Pizza Per Order: The average number of pizza sold per order,calculate by        dividing the 
   total number of pizza sold by the total number of orders.
